We support the development of 21st century skills in young people with a high desire to excel.

About us

Information

  • Tools For Youth El Salvador is a social program that focuses on developing 21st century skills in young people of limited economic resources through training in digital competencies, soft skills, English, reading comprehension, writing and mathematical logic.

Mission

Create spaces for the development and strengthening of key skills that allow Salvadoran youth to access academic and employment opportunities.

Vision

An El Salvador in which all young people are motivated, aspire and have access to opportunities that improve their quality of life and that of their families.

Frequently Asked Questions

1.How can I volunteer with TFY El Salvador?


You can contact us through our social networks or email. We also invite you to keep an eye out for opportunities to join our team that we launch on our social networks.


2.What do we look for in a volunteer?


Volunteers are fundamental to the development of our work. Therefore, we are looking for people with a desire to support the education of other young people, with commitment and professionalism.


3. Who can be part of the TFY El Salvador volunteer team?


Anyone who wishes to join our team is welcome to do so. Our team is free of any kind of discrimination, so if you have the desire and commitment you can join.


4. Where does TFY El Salvador work?


Our work mainly takes place in public schools where there is a population of youth between the ages of 15 and 20. However, we also work with youth in some communities in El Salvador.


5.Is TFY El Salvador a virtual or face-to-face program?


We work mainly in person, but we also develop some virtual activities to reach more young people.


6.What does TFY El Salvador's on-site program consist of?


Our face-to-face programs are designed taking into account the educational needs of the student population we work with in each school or community; however, our workshops focus on developing computer, logic and leadership skills.


7.Is TFY El Salvador linked to any political party?


No. TFY El Salvador is a youth organization with no links to political parties.
